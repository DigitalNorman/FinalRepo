Findings Plan

On the left side there will be the database and on the right side there will be the map which reflects the search results. The plan is to feature a searchable database that includes all 98 entries. The first name, surname, total amount of knights owed, amount of soldiers owed, amount of fiefs held (measured in knights), the medieval name of the commune, the modern commune name, the modern province the commune falls into, and their superior (either a count or constable). You should be able to search through this database and it should project this geographical information onto a map on the righthand side. The major limitation here is that the modern commune and province do not reflect the medieval territorial boundaries. The map should be considered a generalization regarding geographical distribution.
